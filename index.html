THE KNOX SIGNATURE WEDDING EXPERIENCE

A single, cohesive atmosphere that evolves throughout your wedding day.

Knox Signature designs the complete sound and energy of your wedding—from cocktail hour to final song. Rather than treating each moment as a separate service, we approach the evening as one continuous experience with intention, flow, and restraint.

Our role is to shape how the room feels from the first note to the final song.

01 — COCKTAIL HOUR

Live performance that sets the tone.

Cocktail hour is performed live and curated to set the tone for the night.

Phillip performs piano with Ben on saxophone, blending live instrumentation with modern stylings and carefully selected tracks when appropriate. The result is social, elevated, and engaging without feeling like background music.

02 — RECEPTION

The reception unfolds naturally in two phases.

Dinner

  • Thoughtful music selection that supports conversation
  • Seamless handling of toasts using wireless microphones
  • Optional live accents to complement key moments

Dance

  • DJ-led set with live saxophone integration
  • Energy builds intentionally without gimmicks

PRODUCTION & DESIGN

Minimal, architectural, and integrated.

Our setup is designed to be minimal, architectural, and integrated into the space.

This includes:

  • A clean, white DJ command center
  • Premium column-array sound on both sides of the room
  • Architectural lighting to shape the space without overpowering it
  • Wireless microphones and digital mixing for clarity and control

Everything is designed to look as good as it sounds.
